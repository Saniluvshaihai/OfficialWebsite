Year 8

In the Year 8 Electronics course, skills and activities will involve computational thinking, computer aided design, lasercutting, circuit construction, and a project that will incorporate the above to meet a design brief. Solar power and sustainable energy creation will also be key themes. Along with this, the Year 8 students will get to try out Robotics.

Year 9

Design (Technology) is about making a difference in our world - through developing designs, products and systems that meet the requirements of a design brief. The Design Cycle is a key development process that is followed at all year levels in Technology. The Year 9 Digital Technology course starts with an introduction to Digital Design and an exploration what it means to be an informed and responsible Digital Citizen. This is followed by a short unit exploring AI technology. Then we do the main unit, in which students apply the design cycle to design a video game to promote awareness of good cause of their choosing.

Year 10

In the Year 10 In course we aim to instil the idea that our students be creators rather than just consumers of digital technology. In the Digital Technology section of the course, students will be introduced to HTML/CSS and JavaScript code, which will be used to create a fully functioning website. They will also learn about computer programming. In the Electronics section of the course students will gain knowledge and skills of electronic circuitry, basic computer science, and develop computer programming skills which will be applied in a 'good cause' context that may include robotics and the IoT (Internet of Things).
